The KIET group of Institutions or the Krishna Institute of Engineering and Technology, Ghaziabad, was established in 1988. It is affiliated with the Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. KIET offers programs and courses in the technical domains and the institute has been accredited by NAAC with Grade 'A' and its programmes (CSE, ECE, EEE, IT, CE, ME), MCA, MBA and Pharmacy are NBA accredited.

For taking the admission in the institute you have to clear JEE MAINS exam.This institute provides top level of faculties even lot of them are IITians. The institute campus is well developed with latest facilities & modern amenities. The informal and harmonious campus life offers opportunities for the student to pursue their studies, do research work as well as various extra- curricular activities. The campus, situated at NH-58, is conveniently connected to all destinations in Delhi - NCR. To visit official website of college click KIET.
